Generate items using prefix/suffix system.

STRUCTURE:
baseName: single word ("Sword","Staff","Ring")
prefix: {name, type:"prefix", effect} (optional)
suffix: {name, type:"suffix", effect} (optional)

RARITY RULES:
Common/Uncommon/Rare: affixes optional
Epic: needs 1+ affix
Legendary: needs prefix+suffix

EXAMPLES:
• "Flaming" prefix → fire damage
• "of the Bear" suffix → strength
• Epic "Shadow Dagger" = {baseName:"Dagger", prefix:{name:"Shadow",effect:"stealth"}}

CONSUMABLES (hp/gold/xp):
Healing Potion: hp,5-10 | Bandage: hp,2-2 | XP Scroll: xp,20-50
